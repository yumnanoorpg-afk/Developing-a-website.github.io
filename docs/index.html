About Us

Kombu is a Japanese and Korean street food stall based in Stevenage, serving freshly prepared dishes made with quality ingredients and care in every order. Located in the Stevenage Market, we aim to bring bold, authentic flavours into a relaxed and welcoming setting. Our focus is on consistency, freshness, and creating food that people genuinely enjoy coming back for.
